## Hook (the bet)
"You buy a call struck at 100 and *sell* a call struck at 120 вҖ” a bull call spread. The stock rips to S_T = 130. How much does the position pay at expiry?" The naked-call instinct screams *unbounded* (30 and climbing), but the call you **sold** eats every dollar above 120 вҖ” the gain is **capped at KвӮӮвҲ’KвӮҒ = 20**. The bet forces the learner to admit a short leg subtracts, so a multi-leg payoff is the **sum** of its legs, not the long leg alone.

## Core promise (one idea)
**A multi-leg position's payoff is the sum of its legs: `ОЈ qtyВ·max(В·,0)` (short legs carry `qty<0`, so they subtract).** Add the legs and the caps & break-points draw themselves вҖ” a bull spread caps at KвӮӮвҲ’KвӮҒ, a butterfly peaks at the wing width, a straddle/strangle pays on *magnitude*. The same no-arb logic that bounds a single option bounds the spread's price: `0 вүӨ cвӮҒвҲ’cвӮӮ вүӨ KвӮӮвҲ’KвӮҒ`.

## Misconceptions (Specialist вҖ” per-option, feed-forward)
**Bet `opt3-bet` (byOption вҖ” "Bull spread long c(100)/short c(120); payoff at S_T = 130?"):**
- **"30 вҖ” your long call pays 130вҲ’100=30; the upside is yours"** вҶ’ *(a) unbounded.* "Let's test it вҖ” you **sold** the 120 call, so it pays you вҲ’(130вҲ’120) = вҲ’10. Net = 30 вҲ’ 10 = **20**. The short leg caps every dollar above 120." (false)
- **"20 вҖ” the call you sold caps the gain at KвӮӮвҲ’KвӮҒ = 20"** вҶ’ "Good instinct вҖ” above 120 both legs move together and offset dollar-for-dollar, freezing the spread at **20** (#9). Let's prove it leg by leg." (true)
- **"0 вҖ” a long and a short call just cancel"** вҶ’ *(d) over-cancellation / sign.* "Let's test it вҖ” they only offset *above* 120. Between 100 and 120 the long leg is live and the short is still worthless, so the spread pays S_TвҲ’100 there, climbing to its **20** cap (#9)." (false)

**Gate `opt3-gate` (byOption refutations вҖ” foils are EXACTLY `CONFUSABLE[payoff-construction]`):**
- **"PutвҖ“call parity"** вҶ’ "Parity (`C вҲ’ P = S вҲ’ K`) relates a call and a put **price** вҖ” there's no put here and you're asked for the **cash at expiry**, not a price relation. That's a payoff read, not parity." (refutes `put-call-parity`)
- **"No-arbitrage replication"** вҶ’ "Replication builds a stock+bond book to **price** the position. The question wants the **payoff** (what it pays at S_T), which you read directly by summing each leg's `max(В·,0)`." (refutes `no-arbitrage-replication`)
- **"Payoff construction"** вҶ’ correct: add each leg's `max(В·,0)` at the given S_T (short legs subtract). (== `gate.correct` == `schemaId`)

**`opt3-mastery` (butterfly #11 + bound #10):** most likely wrong peak = **20** вҶ’ *(b)* "That's the **outer span** KвӮғвҲ’KвӮҒ = 110вҲ’90. The peak is the **wing width** KвӮӮвҲ’KвӮҒ = 100вҲ’90 = **10** вҖ” the short *middle* pair (вҲ’2В·c(100)) caps it there (#11)." Bound slip: quoting a price > 20 вҶ’ "The payoff never exceeds KвӮӮвҲ’KвӮҒ = 20 and never drops below 0 at **any** S_T, so by no-arbitrage the price is trapped in **[0, 20]** (#10)."

**`transfer-heldout` (#12 strangle):** likely error "it's a directional bet, so 70 pays 0" вҶ’ *(c)* "A strangle (long put 90 + long call 110) is a **magnitude/volatility** bet вҖ” it wins on a big move **either** way. At 70 the put pays 90вҲ’70 = **20**; at 130 the call pays 130вҲ’110 = **20**; only the quiet middle (100) pays **0**." Also the L1вҶ’L3 sign reminder: two **long** legs both *add* (no subtraction here).

**Misconceptions targeted (all wired as bet byOptions and/or gate foils):** (a) unbounded spread payoff вҶ’ short leg caps at KвӮӮвҲ’KвӮҒ; (b) butterfly peak = outer span вҶ’ it's the wing width (10, not 20); (c) straddle/strangle is directional вҶ’ it's a magnitude bet; (d) sign error on short legs (`qty<0` subtracts).
